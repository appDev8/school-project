The Itqan Way
One principle holds everything together — Itqan: excellence in whatever your hands turn to, performed as worship. From that centre, Mind
(‘ilm), Hands (‘amal) and Heart (iman) become one journey, not three subjects. Knowledge becomes action, action is done with excellence,
and the purpose of reading the world (Iqra) is to flourish and serve — which is also the project-based-learning arc of learn → apply → reflect
→ contribute.
