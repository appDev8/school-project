## 2. NSW legal requirements (FACT)
- **Registration (Education Act 1990, incl. s.83):** non-government schools must be **registered** by NESA to operate, and separately **accredited** to present students for RoSA/HSC; monitored against the **Registered and Accredited Individual Non-government Schools (NSW) Manual**. (rego.nesa.nsw.edu.au; legislation.nsw.gov.au)
- **Responsible persons & governance:** must have relevant qualifications/skills/experience, are accountable for proper governance, and must complete **NESA-approved governance training**. (rego.nesa.nsw.edu.au …proper-governance)
- **NESA teacher accreditation:** mandatory at **Graduate** and **Proficient**; all teachers need active accreditation; based on the Australian Professional Standards for Teachers; NESA sole decision-maker since 29 Nov 2022. (nsw.gov.au …nesa/teacher-accreditation)
- **Working With Children Check (WWCC):** valid OCG clearance mandatory for paid child-related work; lasts **5 years**, continuously monitored, **verified via the OCG portal before commencement**; NSW check required (interstate not accepted); cost **$107** paid / free volunteer. (ocg.nsw.gov.au; service.nsw.gov.au)
- **Child Safe Standards / Child Safe Scheme (Children's Guardian Act 2019):** comply with the scheme + implement the **10 Child Safe Standards**, overseen by OCG with enforcement powers. (ocg.nsw.gov.au/child-safe-scheme)

## 4. Educator onboarding & training (REC, sourced models) — Deliverable I
- **Pre-launch induction (2–4 wks):** compliance + culture (Child Safe, WWCC verification, mandatory reporting), tarbiyah/values integration, the NESA Stage 4 outcome map, intro to Manhal.
- **PBL certification:** enrol teaching staff in **PBLWorks (Buck Institute) Gold Standard PBL** — PBL 101 + sustained coaching; consider **PBLWorks TEACH** for ready, standards-aligned projects. *Evidence note:* PBLWorks itself states teachers need **sustained** PD + collaboration time, not a one-off. (pblworks.org)
- **Instructional coaching:** adopt **Jim Knight's Impact Cycle** (Identify → Learn → Improve) + partnership principles; pair each Mentor & Coach with non-evaluative coaching. (instructionalcoaching.com)
- **Fidelity rubric:** build from PBLWorks' **Project Based Teaching Practices**, extended with mastery-gating + advisory criteria; used for self-review/coaching, **not appraisal**.
- **Ongoing cadence:** weekly coaching/critique; fortnightly project-tuning protocols; termly fidelity review; plus **NESA-mandated PD** to maintain Proficient accreditation.

## 5. Industry partnerships (REC, sourced) — Deliverable J
Formalise so partnerships survive staff turnover; research frames them as **MOU-governed**, mutually beneficial, with **mentors as boundary-crossers** + explicit governance/resourcing. (educationandemployers.org; tandfonline.com)
- **MOU components:** shared purpose/outcomes; scope + stream alignment; mentor commitments; **safeguarding clauses (WWCC + Child Safe)**; supervision/duty-of-care + WHS; IP/privacy; insurance; review/exit; a named coordinator each side.
- **Mentor vetting & safeguarding:** every mentor with child-related contact holds a **verified WWCC** + follows the school's Child Safe code; off-site learning needs documented supervision + risk assessment.
- **Matching to streams:** map mentors to the three streams by expertise; partnerships lead curates + QA's matches.
- **Governance:** a partnerships register, periodic MOU review, reporting to Head/board.
